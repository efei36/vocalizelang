Trainer

To use the Trainer, you first record your speech. Then, you type in the phrase you recorded, which the Trainer will read aloud. You can then compare your speech to the Trainer’s.

Listener

The Listener works by listening to your speech, then attempting to recognize and type it. If the Listener detects that you’re mispronouncing something, it will display in the text. Although the Listener uses a top-of-the-line speech-to-text software, it is not always accurate. Please keep in mind that the Listener is not spot-on. Listener is only supported on Chrome browser on Windows PC.

Recorder

The Recorder records your speech and allows you to save it to your disk.
